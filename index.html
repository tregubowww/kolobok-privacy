Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 24, 2026

Short version: Kolobok VPN does not collect, store, or share any personal data or traffic logs. We operate a strict no-log policy.

1. Introduction

Kolobok VPN ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use the Kolobok VPN application ("App").

2. Data We Do NOT Collect

We operate a strict no-log policy. We do not collect, store, or share:

  • Your real IP address
  • VPN IP addresses assigned to you
  • Browsing history or traffic destination
  • DNS queries
  • Timestamps of VPN connections
  • Bandwidth usage logs
  • Any personally identifiable information

3. Data Necessary for Service Operation

To provide VPN service, the App requires the following permissions on your device:

  • VPN permission — to create an encrypted tunnel for your traffic
  • Internet access — to connect to VPN servers
  • Foreground service — to maintain the VPN connection while in use

None of this data is transmitted to us or any third party.

4. Account Information

Kolobok VPN does not require account registration. No email address, phone number, or personal credentials are collected or stored.

5. VPN Server Infrastructure

Our VPN servers are operated on dedicated infrastructure. Server logs are disabled. We cannot technically reconstruct your online activity even if compelled to do so.

6. Third-Party Services

Kolobok VPN does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking services. The App does not contain ads.

7. Children's Privacy

Kolobok VPN is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any significant changes by updating the date at the top of this page.

9. Your Rights

Since we do not collect personal data, there is no personal data to access, correct, or delete. If you have any questions, feel free to contact us.
